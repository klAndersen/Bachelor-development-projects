CleanMyFolder

Have your harddrive started to fill up?
Are your files and folder spread around without having any knowlegde over what's where?
Then this is the program for you.

CleanMyFolder scans your selected location and lists up the folders and files related to your scan.
Then you have the option to either move or delete the selected folder and files.
Clean your folder now!

Features
  • Include or exclude subfolders
  • Scan for files based on filetype
  • Scan for files based on size
  • Scan for files based on date...
    • Created
    • Modified
    • Last accessed
  • Or scan for files based on all above
  • Move file(s) to a new location
  • Move folder and its content to a new location
  • Delete a folder
  • Delete file(s)
